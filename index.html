About

Engineer first.
QA second. Always both.

I started in manual QA and moved fast into automation, not because it was the path of least resistance, but because I saw how much broken software cost teams that shipped without a safety net. I became the person who built that net.

At Integrated Dealer Systems I led Service CRM automation inside a .NET/C# Selenium framework (POM, TDD, NUnit) and brought coverage from near zero to 70%, cutting regression cycle time by 40%. I was the QA point of contact for release management across web, desktop, and mobile.

At Motive I stepped into a high-scale AWS fleet and driver safety platform. I engineered Playwright regression suites, built API automation pipelines with Postman and Newman, and architected the CX Performance Test Suite, a pre-release benchmarking pipeline that measured p50 and p95 response times across critical customer journeys before every single release.

Beyond testing: I have shipped a full-stack mobile app (React Native, Node.js, PostgreSQL) and built a C# .NET data generator with exponential backoff retry logic, SemaphoreSlim parallel execution, and token-based auth. Understanding the system is what makes testing it precise.
